Build your own Gem

A Gem is a custom assistant you configure once and reuse with one click. Go to gemini.google.com → "Explore Gems" → "+ New Gem". Use this 4-part formula in the instructions box.

1Persona
Who it acts as. "You are a product marketer who explains features in plain language."
2Task
What it does each time. "When I paste a spec, produce a customer-ready explainer."
3Context
What it should know. Upload or link brand guidelines & product docs from Drive.
4Format
How the output looks. Spell out the exact sections, length, and tone.
Pro tip: link the Gem to a Google Doc of your product specs or brand voice. When you update the Doc, the Gem uses the new version automatically — no editing the Gem needed.

Habits that keep you safe and sharp

AI gives you a fast first draft, not a finished one. These habits keep the quality — and trust with your customers — high.

🔎

Verify before it leaves your hands

Always fact-check names, numbers, pricing, dates, and product claims before anything reaches a customer. Gemini can sound confident and still be wrong.

🔒

Know your data boundaries

Use Gemini inside your work Workspace account (your data stays governed by your org's controls). Check with your admin before pasting confidential customer data anywhere — and avoid the personal consumer app for sensitive material.

📎

Remember the linked-file limit

In Docs, Gemini summarizes the text in the active document only — it won't pull in a separate appendix or linked file. Bring that content into the doc or attach it to a Gem.

🗣️

Keep your judgment in the loop

You know the relationship, the politics, and what's landed before. Treat drafts as raw material and add the human read the AI can't have.

🔁

Turn winners into Gems

When a prompt gives you a great result twice, save it as a Gem. That's how AI shifts from "a thing you sometimes open" to part of how you work.

📅

Run a 15-minute Friday review

Each week, note one task Gemini saved you time on and one it didn't. Refine the prompt or Gem. Small tweaks compound fast.
