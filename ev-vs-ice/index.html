EV vs ICE

Cost comparison.

EV: an electric vehicle, powered only by a battery and electric motor, charged from the grid. ICE: an internal-combustion-engine vehicle, the conventional car that burns gasoline. This tool compares those two drivetrains only; hybrids and plug-in hybrids, which pair an engine with a battery, are not included.
